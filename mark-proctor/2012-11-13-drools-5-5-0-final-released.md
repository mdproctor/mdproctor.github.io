### [Drools 5.5.0.Final released](<https://blog.kie.org/2012/11/drools-5-5-0-final-released.html>)

by [Mark Proctor](<https://blog.kie.org/category/all?search_authors=3> "Posts by Mark Proctor") \- November 13, 2012  
[Rules](<https://blog.kie.org/category/rules>) [Release](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/release>)

We’re happy to announce the release of Drools (Expert, Fusion, Planner, Guvnor) **5.5.0.Final**.

Documentation, Release Notes and Downloads are detailed below:

  * [Read the new and noteworthy changes here.](<http://docs.jboss.org/drools/release/5.5.0.Final/droolsjbpm-introduction-docs/html/releaseNotes.html#releaseNotes_5.5.0>)

  * Get the latest artifacts from either the [JBoss Maven](<https://repository.jboss.org/nexus/index.html#nexus-search;gav~org.drools*~~5.5.0.Final~~>) or [Maven Central](<http://search.maven.org/#search%7Cga%7CR1%7Corg.drools>)*

  * Download the zips from the bottom of the Drools download [page](<http://www.jboss.org/drools/downloads>). 

  * To try out the examples, just unzip one and run the runExamples.sh or runExamples.bat script.

Try it out and give us some feed-back ([user list](<http://www.jboss.org/drools/lists>), [issue tracker](<https://issues.jboss.org/browse/JBRULES>)).

[* Note: At the time of posting Maven Central has not been synchronized]
