GoogleAlert just let me know that [Nationwide](<http://www.nationwide.com/index.jsp>)  
are doing a Drools presentation at the [Central Ohio Java Users Group](<http://www.cojug.org/>) on the 14th of October. The details can be found [here](<http://www.cojug.org/index.php?option=com_eventlist&Itemid=27&func=details&did=42>), and I’ve pasted them below. It’s really nice to see other members in the community promoting the use of Drools, especially a big brand name like Nationwide:
