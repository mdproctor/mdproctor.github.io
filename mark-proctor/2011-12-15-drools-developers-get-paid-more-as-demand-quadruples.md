Good news for those with JBoss Drools skills, your in demand!!! Latest results in the UK from [itjobswatch.co.uk](<http://www.itjobswatch.co.uk/>) shows over a three times increase in demand for Drools jobs since Dec 2010. It doesn’t take much to imagine where that curve is going to be in 2014…..  
[![](/legacy/assets/images/2011/12/7196d349c4f2-drools-growth.png)](</assets/images/2011/12/a5dbcedd07d6-drools-growth.png>)  
The report for this information can be seen [here](<http://www.itjobswatch.co.uk/jobs/uk/drools.do>).

But it gets better the average salary in London over the last 3 months is £90K GBP that’s around £140K USD. And note that’s just the average, I know I’ve seen jobs over 100K GBP in London for Drools. The demand growth for Drools in London has quadrupled to a 4x increase in the same period as above:  
[![](/legacy/assets/images/2011/12/0a7f3ef4d332-drools-growth-london.png)](</assets/images/2011/12/3c62edeea4b8-drools-growth-london.png>)

Now lets compare this to the average Java developer, the report for that is [here](<http://www.itjobswatch.co.uk/jobs/uk/java.do>). The results are much more depressing, your demand as a java developer is shrinking, with salaries averaging just 55K GBP or 88K USD for London:  
[![](/legacy/assets/images/2011/12/ce70a09363a1-java-growth.png)](</assets/images/2011/12/542e09629967-java-growth.png>)  
So if you are already a fellow Drools developer, congratualations and enjoy the finer things in life. If you aren’t, what are you waiting for, now is the perfect time to dip your toes in – as someone famous once said[ “Because your worth it”](<http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W-LL9z5-nqY>) :)
