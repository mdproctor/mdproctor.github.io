[![](/legacy/assets/images/2012/07/83b29b3cf5dd-Doug_Lenat_150w_220h_2.png)](</assets/images/2012/07/d52cc23abc23-Doug_Lenat_150w_220h_2.png>)I’m very excited that this year [Dr. Douglas Lenat will be one of the keynote Speakers](<http://intellifest.org/wordpress/keynotes/#Doug_Lenat>). Dr Lenat is the founder of the gound breaking AI project [Cyc](<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyc>):  
_“**Cyc** is an [artificial intelligence project](<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_notable_artificial_intelligence_projects> "List of notable artificial intelligence projects") that attempts to assemble a comprehensive [ontology](<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ontology_%28computer_science%29> "Ontology \(computer science\)") and [knowledge base](<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Knowledge_base> "Knowledge base") of everyday [common sense knowledge](<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Common_sense_knowledge> "Common sense knowledge"), with the goal of enabling [AI](<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artificial_intelligence> "Artificial intelligence") applications to perform human-like reasoning.” (wikipedia)_

[![](/legacy/assets/images/2012/07/1c812558e6e4-greenes_caption-news_individual.gif)](</assets/images/2012/07/073d18026cb2-greenes_caption-news_individual.gif>)Dr. Douglas Lenat joins Dr Robert Greenes, a [biomedical and infomatics star](<http://www.flinn.org/news/454>) from Arizona State University, who will be [keynoting the Healthcare day](<http://blog.athico.com/2012/07/intellifest-oct-2012-san-diego.html>).
