Nov 4-6, eBay, San Jose

[Decision Camp](<http://www.blogger.com/blogger.g?blogID=5869426#editor/target=post;postID=5773189504319218657>) is a free conference for Business Analysts and Software Architects. Hosted onsite at eBay, San Jose.

They are still accepting abstracts from speakers, [click here for details](<http://decision-camp.weebly.com/call-for-speakers.html>). There will also be additional vertical tracks, for healthcare and financial services.
