### [Wumpus World Update](<https://blog.kie.org/2012/01/wumpus-world-update.html>)

by [Mark Proctor](<https://blog.kie.org/category/all?search_authors=3> "Posts by Mark Proctor") \- January 26, 2012  
[Rules](<https://blog.kie.org/category/rules>) [Article](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/article>)

I made further progress with Wumpus World today, as previously blogged [here](<http://blog.athico.com/2012/01/wumpus-world.html>). The project is checked into drools-examples, execute “WumpusWorldServer” to run.  
-larger sensor icons  
-bump and scream sensors added  
-left, right buttons now rotate left and rotate right.  
-hero faces direction based on rotation  
-can now shoot arrows  
-wumpus can die
