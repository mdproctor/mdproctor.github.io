### [AI Engineer – Entando are Hiring](<https://blog.kie.org/2017/02/ai-engineer-entando-are-hiring.html>)

by [Mark Proctor](<https://blog.kie.org/category/all?search_authors=3> "Posts by Mark Proctor") \- February 2, 2017  
[General](<https://blog.kie.org/category/general>) [Article](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/article>)

[Entando](<http://www.entando.com/>) are looking to hire an AI Engineer, in Italy, to work closely with the Drools team building a next generation platform for integrated and hybrid AI. Together we’ll be looking at how we can build systems that leverage and integrate different AI paradigms for the [contextual awareness](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Context_awareness>) domain – such as enhancing our complex event processing, building fuzzy/probability rules extensions or looking at Case Based Learning/Reasoning to help with predictive behavioural automation.

The application link can be found [here](<https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/view/252537735/>).
