James Taylor,[ Smart Enough Systems](<http://www.smartenoughsystems.com/>), is going to keynote for us on Wednesday at our Drools meeting starting April 19th, as [blogged](<http://blog.athico.com/2010/03/april-19th-boot-camp-to-be-open-to-all.html>) here. The talk will be titled “Smarter systems for uncertain times”. I’ll blog more details in a few hours.
