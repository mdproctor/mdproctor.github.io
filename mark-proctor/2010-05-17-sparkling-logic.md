Carole-Ann and Carlos, who recently left FICO, have just setup their new venture – [Sparkling Logic](<http://www.sparklinglogic.com/>). Carlos and Carole-Ann have been actively involved in the [October Rules Fest](<http://octoberrulesfest.org/>) from the start, now called [Rules Fest](<http://rulesfest.org/html/home.html>), and I have always enjoyed meeting them there, and I think they are just finally starting to get use to me :) so I wish them the best of luck in their new venture.
