### [Drools&jBPM Drop in Centre at JBoss World (Open to All)](<https://blog.kie.org/2012/06/droolsjbpm-drop-in-centre-at-jboss-world-open-to-all.html>)

by [Mark Proctor](<https://blog.kie.org/category/all?search_authors=3> "Posts by Mark Proctor") \- June 12, 2012  
[Process](<https://blog.kie.org/category/process>) [Rules](<https://blog.kie.org/category/rules>) [Article](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/article>)

Now you have another reason to come to [JBoss World](<http://www.redhat.com/summit/>), as if you didn’t already have too many on your list :)

On Friday the 29th of June we’ll have an all day drop in centre for Drools & jBPM. We’ll be having an all day team meeting and codeathon which will be open to the public. Anyone can attend, you DO NOT need Red HaT Summit tickets to attend. You can come along and hang out with us for the whole day and code, or just drop in for a bit and we’ll help you with your questions.
