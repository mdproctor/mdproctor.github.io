### [Drools and jBPM workshop for Washington DC completley sold out!!! AGAIN!!!!](<https://blog.kie.org/2012/06/drools-and-jbpm-workshop-for-washington-dc-completley-sold-out-again.html>)

by [Mark Proctor](<https://blog.kie.org/category/all?search_authors=3> "Posts by Mark Proctor") \- June 23, 2012  
[Process](<https://blog.kie.org/category/process>) [Rules](<https://blog.kie.org/category/rules>) [Article](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/article>)

After the success of our sold out [Drools & jBPM workshop extravaganza in New York](<http://blog.athico.com/2012/06/drools-and-jbpm-workshop-for-new-york.html>), we thought there was no way it could be topped. We couldn’t be more wrong. The Drools & jBPM workshop in Washington DC a few days later also sold out. With a room capacity of 100, we filled every seat and had to bring in more chairs. The room had large swivel chairs in a half hexagon shape, giving it a “War Room” like feel – how fitting :)

Thanks again for all those that made this possible. We’ll upload [slides soon](<http://www.jboss.org/drools/presentations>).
