### [IntelliFest Drools & jBPM Healthcare Workshop Agenda (San Diego Oct 2012)](<https://blog.kie.org/2012/09/intellifest-drools-jbpm-healthcare-workshop-agenda-san-diego-oct-2012.html>)

by [Mark Proctor](<https://blog.kie.org/category/all?search_authors=3> "Posts by Mark Proctor") \- September 7, 2012  
[Rules](<https://blog.kie.org/category/rules>) [Article](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/article>)

[Yesterday I posted](<http://blog.athico.com/2012/09/intellifest-drools-jbpm-workshop-agenda.html>) the general Drools & jBPM work shop agenda. This year there is an additional day dedicated to Healthcare professionals, with top [keynote speaker Dr Robert Greenes](<http://blog.athico.com/2012/07/intellifest-oct-2012-san-diego.html>).

[![](/legacy/assets/images/2012/09/02b0fca20e17-featured_item_11-625x220.png)![](/legacy/assets/images/2012/09/02b0fca20e17-featured_item_11-625x220.png)](<http://intellifest.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/featured_item_11-625x220.png>)

The Healthcare day is being chaired by Captain Emory Fry, MD and Dr Davide Sottara.

[**Monday 22nd October, Bahia Resort Hotel, San Diego.**](<http://intellifest.org/wordpress/>) Free Entrance to first 100 registrations.

**0830-0920** Captain Emory Fry, MD  
**Socratic Grid : Open Source Distributed Decision Support for Healthcare**  
  
**0930-1020** [Dr Robert Greenes](<https://web59.asu.edu/directory/person/1114610>), Department of Biomedical Informatics, Arizona State University  
**Key Note : Embedding Decision Support in Clinical Systems**  
  
**1030-1120** Dr [Aziz Boxwala](<http://dbmi.ucsd.edu/confluence/display/DBMI/Aziz+Boxwala,+MD,+PhD>), Division of Biomedical Informatics, University of California  
**Clinical Decision Support Consortium**  
  
**1130-1200** [Dr ](<http://www.osehra.org/blog/221>)[Seong Ki Mun](<http://www.osehra.org/blog/221>), President and CEO of OSEHRA  
**Open Source Electronic Health Record Agent**  
  
**1200:1300** Lunch  
  
**1300-1350** [Dr Davide Sottara](<http://www.linkedin.com/in/davidesottara>), Department of Biomedical Informatics, Arizona State University  
**Knowledge Representation Standards For Building Executable Clinical Processes Models**  
  
**1400-1450** [David Shields](<https://sites.google.com/a/opencds.org/opencds/collaborators>), Health ITS, University of Utah  
**OpenCDS: a Clinical Decision Support Infrastructure Based on Drools**  
  
**1500-1520**[Harold Solbring](<http://www.linkedin.com/pub/harold-solbrig/0/1a7/576>), Division of Biomedical Informatics, Mayo Clinic  
**Semantic Wiki**  
  
**1530-1655** [Conor Dowling](<http://www.caregraf.com/#conorposts>), Caregraf  
**Semantic Terminology Services**  
  
**1600-1620** [Dr ](<http://www.bioeng.ucla.edu/people/faculty/affiliate-faculty-1/xiao-hu-phd>)[Xiao Hu](<http://www.bioeng.ucla.edu/people/faculty/affiliate-faculty-1/xiao-hu-phd>), Department of Neurosurgery, University of California  
**Clinical Decision Support Driven Alarms**  
  
**1630-1700**  
**Panel Discussion – Collaboration Opportunities**
