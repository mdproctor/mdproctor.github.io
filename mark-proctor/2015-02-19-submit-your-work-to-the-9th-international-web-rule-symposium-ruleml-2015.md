### [Submit your work to the 9th International Web Rule Symposium (RuleML 2015)](<https://blog.kie.org/2015/02/submit-your-work-to-the-9th-international-web-rule-symposium-ruleml-2015.html>)

by [Mark Proctor](<https://blog.kie.org/category/all?search_authors=3> "Posts by Mark Proctor") \- February 19, 2015  
[Rules](<https://blog.kie.org/category/rules>) [Article](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/article>)

[Adrian Paschke](<https://www.linkedin.com/profile/view?id=7865606&goback=.gde_2190838_member_5973784447361785857> "Adrian Paschke")Professor at Freie Universitaet Berlin

Dear CEP Colleagues,

I would like to encourage you to submit your research in the field of rules, rule-based reasoning and its applications to RuleML 2015 ([http://2015.ruleml.org](<https://www.linkedin.com/redirect?url=http%3A%2F%2F2015.ruleml.org&urlhash=1V-e&_t=tracking_anet>)).

It is an excellent opportunity for a high impact conference publication ([http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RuleML_Symposium](<https://www.linkedin.com/redirect?url=http%3A%2F%2Fen.wikipedia.org%2Fwiki%2FRuleML_Symposium&urlhash=VNO_&_t=tracking_anet>); e.g., RuleML is in the top 100 venues for impact factor in CiteSeerX [http://citeseerx.ist.psu.edu/stats/venues](<https://www.linkedin.com/redirect?url=http%3A%2F%2Fciteseerx.ist.psu.edu%2Fstats%2Fvenues&urlhash=-70F&_t=tracking_anet>)).

Also note, that there are several additional collocated events, e.g., the Recommender Systems for the Web of Data Challenge ([http://2015.ruleml.org/recsysrules-2015.html](<https://www.linkedin.com/redirect?url=http%3A%2F%2F2015.ruleml.org%2Frecsysrules-2015.html&urlhash=xRuq&_t=tracking_anet>)), the Doctoral Consortium ([http://2015.ruleml.org/DoctoralConsortium.html](<https://www.linkedin.com/redirect?url=http%3A%2F%2F2015.ruleml.org%2FDoctoralConsortium.html&urlhash=KNbX&_t=tracking_anet>)), the 9th International Rules Challenge with competitive prizes for the best rule base, the Reasoning Web Summer School, RR 2015 and the Workshop on Formal Ontologies meet Industry ([http://www.csw.inf.fu-berlin.de/fomi2015/](<https://www.linkedin.com/redirect?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.csw.inf.fu-berlin.de%2Ffomi2015%2F&urlhash=AwdK&_t=tracking_anet>)), as well as the 25th CADE 2015.

If you are doing your Phd in this field I would like to point you to the Reasoning Web Summer School ([http://www.csw.inf.fu-berlin.de/rw2015/](<https://www.linkedin.com/redirect?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.csw.inf.fu-berlin.de%2Frw2015%2F&urlhash=pa1V&_t=tracking_anet>)) and the joint RuleML/RR Doctoral Consortium (([http://2015.ruleml.org/DoctoralConsortium.html](<https://www.linkedin.com/redirect?url=http%3A%2F%2F2015.ruleml.org%2FDoctoralConsortium.html&urlhash=KNbX&_t=tracking_anet>)), where you can submit your Phd paper. Accepted RuleML Phd papers and demo papers will be published in the Challenge proceedings which are listed in DBLP ([http://dblp1.uni-trier.de/db/conf/ruleml/](<https://www.linkedin.com/redirect?url=http%3A%2F%2Fdblp1.uni-trier.de%2Fdb%2Fconf%2Fruleml%2F&urlhash=Vn3G&_t=tracking_anet>)) and fully indexed e.g. in Scopus.  
Also the workshop on Formal Ontologies meet Industry (FOMI 2015) might be relevant for you if you are working on Ontologies ([http://www.csw.inf.fu-berlin.de/fomi2015/](<https://www.linkedin.com/redirect?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.csw.inf.fu-berlin.de%2Ffomi2015%2F&urlhash=AwdK&_t=tracking_anet>)).

And, further interesting things will happen at RuleML 2015, such as an ISO Common Logic, OMG API4KB and OASIS LegalRuleML face-to-face meeting. We will also have a Berlin Semantic Web Meetup during RuleML on August 4th. Details will follow soon on the Meetup website: [http://www.meetup.com/The-Berlin-Semantic-Web-Meetup-Group/](<https://www.linkedin.com/redirect?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.meetup.com%2FThe-Berlin-Semantic-Web-Meetup-Group%2F&urlhash=Vt_o&_t=tracking_anet>).

Thank you and hope to see you in Berlin, Germany in August,

Adrian  
(General Chair RuleML 2015)

Prof. Dr. Adrian Paschke  
AG Corporate Semantic Web  
Freie Universitaet Berlin  
Germany  
[http://www.mi.fu-berlin.de/en/inf/groups/ag-csw/](<https://www.linkedin.com/redirect?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.mi.fu-berlin.de%2Fen%2Finf%2Fgroups%2Fag-csw%2F&urlhash=t5Dv&_t=tracking_anet>)
