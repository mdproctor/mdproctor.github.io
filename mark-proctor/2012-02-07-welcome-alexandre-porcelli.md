### [Welcome Alexandre Porcelli](<https://blog.kie.org/2012/02/welcome-alexandre-porcelli.html>)

by [Mark Proctor](<https://blog.kie.org/category/all?search_authors=3> "Posts by Mark Proctor") \- February 7, 2012  
[General](<https://blog.kie.org/category/general>) [Article](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/article>)

Alexandre Porcelli has joined the Drools&jBPM team today. Alexandre is an [Antlr ](<http://www.antlr.org/>)guru and also leads the Open Spotlight project, <http://porcelli.github.com/OpenSpotLight/>.

Alexendre will initially be helping out on the Guvnor work for jBPM, then in a few months time we hope that he’ll lead our work around distributed computing.

Alexendre lives in sunny Brazil, you can read more about him [here,http://porcelli.com.br/](<http://www.blogger.com/here,http://porcelli.com.br/>).
