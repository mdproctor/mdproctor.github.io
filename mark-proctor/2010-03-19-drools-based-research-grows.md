### [Drools based research grows](<https://blog.kie.org/2010/03/drools-based-research-grows.html>)

by [Mark Proctor](<https://blog.kie.org/category/all?search_authors=3> "Posts by Mark Proctor") \- March 19, 2010  
[Rules](<https://blog.kie.org/category/rules>) [Article](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/article>)

I went to update the [research network page](<http://www.jboss.org/drools/research-network.html>), we had a smattering of research projects involving drools when I updated it last over 15 months ago. I was amazed by the number of research projects now coming up on [google scholar](<http://scholar.google.co.uk/scholar?hl=en&q=drools+OR+%22jboss+rules%22>). I only had time to update some of them, and there is still atleast another third more to go, maybe more. It’s great to see Drools used so exstensively and with such a wide range of topics in academia. It makes us in the Drools team very proud.
