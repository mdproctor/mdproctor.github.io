### [Drools : Towards computerizing intensive care sedation guidelines:design of a rule-based architecture for automated execution of clinical guidelines](<https://blog.kie.org/2010/01/drools-towards-computerizing-intensive-care-sedation-guidelinesdesign-of-a-rule-based-architecture-for-automated-execution-of-clinical-guidelines.html>)

[Rules](<https://blog.kie.org/category/rules>) [Article](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/article>)

[Towards computerizing intensive care sedation guidelines:design of a rule-based architecture for automated execution of clinical guidelines](<http://7thspace.com/headlines/331660/towards_computerizing_intensive_care_sedation_guidelinesdesign_of_a_rule_based_architecture_for_automated_executionof_clinical_guidelines.html>)

“The aim is to close the gap in communication between the IT and the medical domain. This leads to a less time-consuming and error-prone development phase and a shorter clinical evaluation phase.

Methods: A framework is proposed that semi-automatically translates a clinical guideline, expressed as an XML-based flow chart, into a Drools Rule Flow by employing semantic technologies such as ontologies and SWRL.”
