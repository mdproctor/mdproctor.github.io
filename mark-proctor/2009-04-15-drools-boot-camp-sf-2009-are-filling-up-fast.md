### [Drools Boot Camp (SF 2009) are filling up fast](<https://blog.kie.org/2009/04/drools-boot-camp-sf-2009-are-filling-up-fast.html>)

by [Mark Proctor](<https://blog.kie.org/category/all?search_authors=3> "Posts by Mark Proctor") \- April 15, 2009  
[Rules](<https://blog.kie.org/category/rules>) [Article](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/article>)

Places for the boot camp in San Francisco this June are filling up fast, and the Hilton has just increased it’s room rate – the cheapest room has moved from $103 to $127.

Details of the Boot Camp can be found [here](<http://blog.athico.com/2009/03/drools-boot-camp-san-francisco-june.html>).

We are still looking for more people to do lightening talks, so if you’d like to get involved please do let me know, mproctor at redhat d0t com.
