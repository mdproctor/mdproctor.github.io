[Rules](<https://blog.kie.org/category/rules>) [Article](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/article>)

[Third Pillar](<http://thirdpillar.com/>) just came through and offered to sponsor the meeting room. I’m just waiting on my managers approval, but hopefully tomorrow I can put up a blog confirming the Boot Camp will go ahead with dates and times. Now all we need are sponsors for sundries, like a constant stream of coffee and pizza :)

[![](/legacy/assets/images/2009/03/123302abfeb4-SFOFHHH_Hilton_San_Francisco_home_right.jpg)](<http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_Jrhwx8X9P7g/Sb6flGYQOdI/AAAAAAAAATU/d7hxc_iVCrQ/s1600-h/SFOFHHH_Hilton_San_Francisco_home_right.jpg>)  
Initial dates look likely to co-incide with JavaOne, as I’m presenting there. So from the 1st to the 7th of June, inclusive, and the location is looking likely to be the Hilton, where rooms are currently $103 through Expedia.
