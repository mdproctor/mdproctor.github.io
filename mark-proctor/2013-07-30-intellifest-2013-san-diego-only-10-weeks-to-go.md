### [IntelliFest 2013 (San Diego) – only 10 weeks to go](<https://blog.kie.org/2013/07/intellifest-2013-san-diego-only-10-weeks-to-go.html>)

by [Mark Proctor](<https://blog.kie.org/category/all?search_authors=3> "Posts by Mark Proctor") \- July 30, 2013  
[Rules](<https://blog.kie.org/category/rules>) [Article](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/article>)

Here is the updated [flyer for IntelliFest 2013](<http://intellifest.org/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2013/docs/2013_Flyer.pdf>), this October in San Diego. [Dr. Ernest Friedman-Hill](<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ernest_Friedman-Hill>), the creator of [Jess](<http://www.jessrules.com/>), will be there talking about the new Jess 8.0 release. Also [Dr. Daniel Miranker](<http://www.cs.utexas.edu/~miranker/>), the creator of the [Leaps](<http://citeseerx.ist.psu.edu/viewdoc/download?doi=10.1.1.96.5371&rep=rep1&type=pdf>) algorithm (a lazy alternative to the Rete algorithm), will be there talking about web scale distributed pattern matching.

I’ll also be there presenting my new algorithm, entitled [“From Eager to Lazy : The Evolution of the Rete Algorithm”](<http://intellifest.org/wordpress/abstracts/>)
