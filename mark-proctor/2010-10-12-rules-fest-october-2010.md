Rules Fest is in full swing, <http://rulesfest.org/html/home.html>. Great attendance with around 100 people and great food :)

Myself, Edson and Davide are here. Davide did a great lunch time talk. Kris will be arriving on Wednesday to help with the BootCamp.

It’s been a lot of preparation last week and we’ll be very busy this week and next, so apologies if our responses aren’t timely on mailing lists and irc.
