### [Guvnor translations move to Zanata](<https://blog.kie.org/2012/06/guvnor-translations-move-to-zanata.html>)

by [Mark Proctor](<https://blog.kie.org/category/all?search_authors=3> "Posts by Mark Proctor") \- June 6, 2012  
[Tools](<https://blog.kie.org/category/tools>) [Article](<https://blog.kie.org/content_type/article>)

We have completed the preliminary steps to move Guvnor translations to [Zanata](<https://translate.jboss.org/>).

This should open up the possibility for the community to contribute translations more easily.

Getting started couldn’t be any more simple: register your JBoss login at Zanata and request membership to one of the language translation teams. More details are available [here](<https://community.jboss.org/docs/DOC-16540?uniqueTitle=false>).

It’s early days and we no doubt have a lot to learn how best to integrate translations made in Zanata into our continuous integration processes. For the time being we plan to only pull translations from Zanata during the release cycle.

Come join the party, feedback and help make Guvnor better.
