[RuleML](<http://www.ruleml.org/>), [Web Rules and Reasoning](<http://www.rr-conference.org/>) as well as [DecisionCamp](<https://dmcommunity.org/>) are all coming together (as well as being collocated with [BICOD](<http://www.dcs.bbk.ac.uk/bicod17/>)) this year in London.  
RuleML+RR [home](<http://2017.ruleml-rr.org/>), [schedule](<http://2017.ruleml-rr.org/schedule/>), [registration](<https://www2.bbk.ac.uk/ruleml-rr2017/>)  
DecisionCamp [home](<http://2017.ruleml-rr.org/decisioncamp-2017>), [schedule](<http://2017.ruleml-rr.org/decisioncamp-2017/decisioncamp-2017-schedule/>), [registration](<https://www2.bbk.ac.uk/ruleml-rr2017/decisioncamp2017.html>)

Explore that latest AI happenings at RuleML+RR and keep up to date with the latest Decision Model and Notation (DMN) at Decision Camp.

**When:**|  July 12-14 2017  
---|---  
**Where:**|  Birkbeck, University of London, London, UK  
Malet St, London WC1E 7HX, UK  
  
A number of Red Hat Engineers will be there and presenting:  
Mark Proctor – Drools co-founder, BRMS and BPMS Platform Architect:

[The Effectiveness of DMN for Cross Vendor Interoperability](<http://2017.ruleml-rr.org/schedule/>)

Edson Tirelli – Drools project lead: [DMN Technology Compatibility Kit (TCK)](<http://2017.ruleml-rr.org/decisioncamp-2017/decisioncamp-2017-schedule/>), [Demystifying the Decision Model and Notation Specification](<http://2017.ruleml-rr.org/decisioncamp-2017/decisioncamp-2017-schedule/>)  
Geoffrey De Smet – OptaPlanner founder, project lead: [Real-time Constraint Solving with OptaPlanner](<http://2017.ruleml-rr.org/decisioncamp-2017/decisioncamp-2017-schedule/>)

**DecisionCamp:**  
“DecisionCAMP-2017 will include presentations from leading decision management authorities, vendors, and practitioners. The event will explore the current state in Decision Management, the real-world use of the DMN standard, and solutions to various business problems using Decision Management tools and capabilities. The event will include a special Open Discussion “What you Like and What you Do Not Like about DMN” and a QnA Panel “Real-world Business Decision Management: Vendor and Practitioner Perspectives”.  
**RuleML+RR:**  
“2017 is the leading international joint conference in the field of rule-based reasoning, and focuses on theoretical advances, novel technologies, as well as innovative applications concerning knowledge representation and reasoning with rules.”
